About Me

Hi, my name is Aryon, I’ve always wanted to code programs of my own since I was a kid. I have been taking many steps to my goal of a career in programming of pretty much any sort. From websites to programs, I want to learn it all and I have been working hard to do that. I’ve taken several classes in the realm of coding, such as: Video Game design (Hempfield High School 11th Grade), Interactive Media and Web Design (Lancaster County Career and Technical Center), and currently Computer Software Engineering Technology (Thaddeus Stevens College).

As for who I am and my personality, I take my work very seriously and complete work on time. I try to help out with extra work often, especially if I’m all caught up with my own work. I view myself as a friendly person with an outgoing personality. When it comes to dealing with customers, I am able to speak clearly to them and complete their request with ease. I tend to keep solutions and designs simple when completing projects or tasks.
